Healthy made easy

Healthy. For all lifestyles.

Macro Foods is a venue designed for ease and convenience to a healthy lifestyle. We use fresh ingredients to make meals that are never frozen and we cook everything locally right here in Windsor, Ontario. Whether your goals are to slim down, bulk up, or just maintain, our meals are packed with all the necessary macros and micro nutrients to help you out.

Simple. To save you time.

With days seemingly shorter and schedules more packed, we know it can be hard to find time to make a proper meal that not only fills our macros, but also tastes great. We offer a simple 2-step solution. Simply order your meals with our online form, then wait as we deliver your freshly made meals right to your doorstep.

Delicious. Made with fresh ingredients.

We make meal prep not only easy, but tasty too! We use fresh ingredients to cook our meals locally in Windsor and we never freeze our meals. We create our own spice blends and sauces with no additives, fillers, preservatives or MSG so what you see is what you get and taste. With new combinations and flavours weekly, our menu keeps up to date and fresh to keep your taste buds guessing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I pick up my order instead of having it delivered?

Yes, our kitchen is located at 1389 Wyandotte St. E., N9A 3K8 and we're open from 6pm to 9pm.

What time will my meal be delivered?

On Sundays and Wednesdays, we deliver between 12pm and 5pm EST. If the meal was pre-paid for and no one is home, we will leave the meals at the door.

How do I pay for my meal?

We accept cash and credit cards for deliveries and pick ups. If you wish to pre-pay for your meal, please note it in the request section of the form and we will e-mail you an invoice.

Which locations do you deliver to?

As of now, we deliver meals to Windsor, LaSalle, Tecumseh, Walkerville, and the Essex area

What if I have allergies?

We can make special accomodations for you as long as you let us know by filling out the request box in the order form.

What if I want a different plan?

We definitely cater to customized meal plans. Let us know what you were thinking by filling out the request box, and we'll find something that works for you.

Is there a menu?

In the traditional sense of the word, we do not have an offical 'menu' to order from. Meals include a combination of three items (proteins, carbohydrates, or vegetables) that are crafted by our chefs. If you want a specific protein (steak, chicken, shrimp or salmon), or to avoid a certain item, please include it in the request box when ordering.

Do you make vegetarian or vegan meals?

Yes! If you have specific requests that do not fit our basic meal plans, please fill out the request box in the order form and we will work something out.

How do I warm up my food?

All of our meals come delivered in handy packages that can be refigerated and microwaved.

What are Macros?

Macros are short for macronutrients. They are essential nutrients like proteins, carbohydrates, and fats that provide us with the necessary energy to function and grow. You may hear the saying, “Hitting your macros” when talking about fitness and this means to monitor your intake of these macronutrients to keep the proper balance of nutrients in your body. Macro Foods aims to make it as easy as possible to help you with your fitness goals so you can consistently hit your macros.

Do you do catering?

Yes! We cater for multiple occasions big and small. Contact us for more details.
