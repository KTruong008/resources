About Us

Windsor Roots

Macro Foods was designed as a venue for ease and convenience to a healthy lifestyle. Our mission is health made easy. With days seemingly shorter and schedules grossly more full there seems to be one common denominator that gets left behind; packing a lunch. The obesity epidemic is on the rise largely due to these fast choices that we are all forced to make on a day-to-day basis, what should I grab to eat today?

Our Values

Macro Foods is the solution. Our gourmet health food is cooked fresh daily and will leave you wondering how healthy ever tasted so good. We offer a range of health foods and general/custom plans and can work with whatever dietary requirements needed. Contact us today and have your meals delivered right to your door. Need a meal on the go? Stop by our storefront and grab a premade meal. Whatever the case may be let us help you make the changes you want.
